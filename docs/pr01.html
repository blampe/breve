Preface

This is the documentation for The breve Simulation Envionment.

You can also download the breve 2.5.1 documentation in HTML format [808K tar/gz file].

To report bugs in breve, you can either email the author, or post your bug in the bugs section of the breve discussion forum.

Further questions

If you have further questions about breve that aren't answered in the documentation:

  • Try asking your questions in the breve discussion forums

  • Send mail to the author of breve
